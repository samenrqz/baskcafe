Life at Bask

Why Work at Bask Café?

We don't just hire baristas — we grow people. At Bask, every team member is valued, trained, and given room to flourish.

Free Barista Training
All new hires receive structured barista training through our Bask Barista Program.
Free Daily Coffee
One complimentary drink per shift — because you deserve great coffee too.
Growth Path
Clear promotion tracks from Barista → Senior Barista → Manager.
Tight-Knit Team
We're a small, close team that celebrates each other — work feels less like work here.
Flexible Scheduling
We work with your schedule — especially helpful for students and part-timers.
Values-Aligned Work
We source ethically and operate sustainably — work for a brand you're proud of.
Staff Discounts
20% off all menu items — including food, drinks, and special menu additions.
Recognition Programs
Monthly "Barista of the Month" awards with bonuses and social media spotlights.
