Life at Bask

Why Work at Bask Café?

We don't just hire baristas — we grow people. At Bask, every team member is valued, trained, and given room to flourish.

Free Barista Training
All new hires receive structured barista training through our Bask Barista Program.
Free Daily Coffee
One complimentary drink per shift — because you deserve great coffee too.
Growth Path
Clear promotion tracks from Barista → Senior Barista → Manager.
Tight-Knit Team
We're a small, close team that celebrates each other — work feels less like work here.
Flexible Scheduling
We work with your schedule — especially helpful for students and part-timers.
Values-Aligned Work
We source ethically and operate sustainably — work for a brand you're proud of.
Staff Discounts
20% off all menu items — including food, drinks, and special menu additions.
Recognition Programs
Monthly "Barista of the Month" awards with bonuses and social media spotlights.

Application Process

How to Apply

Our hiring process is straightforward and respectful of your time. Here's what to expect:

1

Submit Your Application

Fill in the application form on this page or email your resume to careers@baskcafe.com with the subject line: Position — Your Name

2

Initial Review

Our manager reviews all applications within 3–5 business days. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via email or phone.

3

Interview & Skills Assessment

A brief in-person interview at the café. For barista roles, you'll do a short practical assessment — don't worry, we make it comfortable.

4

Job Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive a written offer, followed by a structured 3-day onboarding with our Bask Barista Training Program.
